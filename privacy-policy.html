Privacy Policy

At Webscripts, safeguarding the privacy and rights of our customers is paramount. We (referred to as "the Site," "Service," "Our," or "Ours") are dedicated to ensuring the privacy of the information our customers entrust to us, utilizing it transparently and responsibly.

Respecting the privacy of our website users, we are committed to shielding your personal information from unauthorized access. We only gather personal data essential for delivering our services and enhancing our website, ensuring it's collected, utilized, and stored in compliance with applicable laws.

This page outlines our approach to collecting, using, and disclosing personal information obtained from site users. By using the site, you consent to the practices detailed in this privacy policy.

Collection and Utilization of Personal Information:
We collect and securely store the personal information you provide voluntarily, such as your name, email address, or phone number. We do not share this information with third parties without your explicit consent, unless necessary for providing our services. Rest assured, we do not employ this data for any purposes other than those for which it was gathered.

We employ robust security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, treating your personally identifiable information with the utmost confidentiality.

Disclosure to Third Parties:
We do not engage in the sale, trade, or rental of your personal information. However, we may disclose your data if required by law or if we believe in good faith that such action is necessary to protect our legal rights.

Data Security:
While we endeavour to employ commercially acceptable methods to safeguard your personal information, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is entirely secure.

Access to Your Personal Information:
You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you. We may request additional information to verify your identity before granting access. If access cannot be provided due to legal reasons or other constraints, we will promptly inform you and provide an explanation.

Cookies and Browsing Data:
Our site utilizes "cookies" to enhance your browsing experience. You have the option to decline these cookies by adjusting your browser settings, though doing so may impact certain site features.

We collect browsing data through cookies and similar technologies to personalize and refine your experience on our website, understand and analyze site traffic and usage data, and identify and address security and usage concerns.

Changes to This Privacy Policy:
This privacy policy is effective as of the specified date and will remain in effect, barring any future revisions, which will take effect immediately upon posting on this page. We reserve the right to update or modify our privacy policy at any time. Your continued use of our service following the posting of any changes to this policy signifies your acceptance of said changes.
